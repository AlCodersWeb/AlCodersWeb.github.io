Know about ATL

Atal Tinkering Labs (ATL) are dynamic spaces in schools across India where students unleash their creativity and dive into hands-on innovation. It's where curiosity meets technology, and the next generation of inventors begins their journey.

What Things can I learn from AlCoders?

We teach the students how to build and program robots,how to debugg and rectify the error ,create 3D designs and print them, and even connect devices through the Internet of Things (IoT). They get hands-on experience with coding, electronics, and cutting-edge technologies like artificial intelligence, all while solving real-world problems through creative projects. These labs emphasize teamwork, ethical thinking, and sustainable innovation, helping students not just to learn but to think critically and inventively about the future.
